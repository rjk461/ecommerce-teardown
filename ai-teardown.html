FAQ

A few common questions before you buy.

What counts as “per page”?

One URL (one page) per report. If you want multiple pages reviewed, submit each URL separately.

What will the report focus on?

Conversion clarity (message-match), trust & proof, CTA strength, friction, mobile usability, hierarchy/attention, and persuasion gaps — with prioritized actions.

How fast is delivery?

Typically within minutes. If the page blocks automated screenshots or is highly dynamic, delivery may take longer or require a manual retry.

Can you review pages behind login?

Not in the automated flow. For gated pages, use the contact form and we can discuss options.
